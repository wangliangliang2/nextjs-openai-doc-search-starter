#### 报错：error: "The original image abnormal"
> imageMogr2将gif转为web并且缩放时，使用imageMogr2/format/webp|imageMogr2/thumbnail/100x报错

**报错链接** ：[https://dn-odum9helk.qbox.me/test.gif?imageMogr2/format/webp|imageMogr2/thumbnail/100x](https://dn-odum9helk.qbox.me/test.gif?imageMogr2/format/webp%7CimageMogr2/thumbnail/100x)

![blob.png](https://dn-odum9helk.qbox.me/Fl5eNfaKeFhQ2Wvh6jnRWfeAE-Sn)

**报错原因** ：imageMogr2参考工具[imageMagick](http://www.imagemagick.com.cn/ "imagemagick")，由于 imageMagick 对 animated webp 进行缩放处理不支持，导致在imageMogr2将.gif转成.webp格式的文件时缩放报错

**解决方法** ：2种。

1）使用管道符，使用imageMogr2缩放.gif图片后再转成.webp格式文件

如：[https://dn-odum9helk.qbox.me/test.gif?imageMogr2/thumbnail/100x|imageMogr2/format/webp](https://dn-odum9helk.qbox.me/test.gif?imageMogr2/thumbnail/100x%7CimageMogr2/format/webp)

![blob.png](https://dn-odum9helk.qbox.me/FspfjQHf8LQW0XPCyHD77KEB_7UA)

2）不使用管道符，在缩放.gif图片时直接指定目标格式为.webp，

如：[https://dn-odum9helk.qbox.me/test.gif?imageMogr2/thumbnail/100x/format/webp](https://dn-odum9helk.qbox.me/test.gif?imageMogr2/thumbnail/100x/format/webp)

![blob.png](https://dn-odum9helk.qbox.me/FnTR-neYs6nvRC6qbwIfU_uq7on0)
